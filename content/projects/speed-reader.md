## About project

Speed Reader is a lightweight RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) speed reading Progressive Web App. It helps users consume text content at a rapid speed by displaying one word at a time in the center of the screen, which reduces eye movement and eliminates subvocalization.
